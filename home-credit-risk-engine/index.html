01 — The Business Case

1 in 12 loans is quietly heading toward default.

Home Credit provides essential financial services to populations often ignored by traditional banking—individuals with minimal formal credit histories. While this mission drives financial inclusion, it introduces a severe systemic risk.

"Lending capital to those who cannot repay it does not help them. It simply delays distress while quietly eroding the institution's portfolio."

Our rigorous analysis across 307,511 loan applications verified that exactly 8.07% of borrowers ultimately default. This is not a statistical anomaly; it is a profound structural pattern embedded deep within the data.

Assuming an average observed loan value of $29,000 USD, an 8% default rate exposes the institution to approximately $718 million in at-risk principal—before any algorithmic intervention.

02 — Signals in the Noise

Five signals that predict a default before it happens.

By synthesizing over 3.3 million rows of external bureau history and internal applications, our distributed PySpark pipeline surfaced the underlying predictors of default. The model operates beyond human intuition, identifying complex, non-linear relationships.

  • External Credit Score (Bureau 3) Independent risk rating from external bureaus.
    25.4%
  • External Credit Score (Bureau 2) Secondary independent credit assessment.
    24.1%
  • Applicant Age (Days Birth) Older applicants statistically demonstrate significantly higher repayment consistency.
    5.1%
  • Previous Refused Count ✦ Engineered The volume of prior rejections. Mined from 1.6M internal historical records.
    4.3%
  • Credit Term Months ✦ Engineered Calculated loan horizon. Longer terms heavily correlate with eventual distress.
    2.7%

Recommended Strategic Actions

Mandate Bureau Data

With external scores driving ~50% of the predictive weight, applications lacking these metrics must forcibly trigger manual senior underwriter escalation.

Tiered Horizons

High-risk applicants flagged by the engine should face restricted, shorter initial loan terms to mitigate the risk associated with prolonged exposure (Credit Term Months).

Flag Historic Friction

Leverage the engineered `PREV_REFUSED` capability to instantly red-flag returning applicants who exhibit chronic behavioral instability across our network.
